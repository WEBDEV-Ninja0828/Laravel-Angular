Promotional Services

Many artists are familiar with reposting on Soundcloud as a form of promotion, which can be a great way of getting your music out there... but wouldn't you like more than this?

Although this is a feature that Sore Thumb Media can offer, our aim is to direct a fresh audience to our site where more promotional attractions can be found and also where you can build a whole new network of fans. We will actively push tracks and even feature those which we believe stick out like a sore thumb. Furthermore, we hope to ultimately become a bridge between an outstanding artist and record labels which could kick off the start of a musical career.

We currently offer:

  • A music discovery section
  • Remix competitions
  • Sore Thumb Media releases

and finally, for the very best track submissions we receive, we will offer a music video service.
